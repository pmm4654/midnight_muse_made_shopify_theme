## Why
Two product templates now exist:
- **Default** (`product.json`) — includes photo upload widget, "What Makes a Purrfect Photo?" guide, and photo-specific FAQ

## How
Shopify Admin → Products → [product] → scroll to **Theme template** dropdown → select **no-upload**
